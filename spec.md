# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a single-page landing page for the Sankalp Garib Kalyan Yojana scheme by Sankalp Marg Foundation, showcasing key welfare services and contact information.

**Planned changes:**
- Fixed top navigation header with logo, nav links (Home, Scheme Info, Contact), clickable phone number in orange, and an orange rounded "Register" pill button
- Full-width hero section with orange-to-white gradient, headline, description, "Apply Now" (orange) and "Call Now" (green) CTA buttons, and a hero community image on the right
- Services section ("Our Key Services") with four cards in a responsive grid: Free Healthcare, Quality Education, Farmer Welfare, Employment Mission — each with an orange icon, bold title, description, and hover effect
- Dark footer (slate-900) with three columns: logo/tagline, quick links, contact details (email, phone, address with icons), and a copyright line
- Consistent visual theme: orange (#ea580c) for accents/CTAs, bold headings, rounded cards/buttons, smooth scroll behavior

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors can learn about the Sankalp Garib Kalyan Yojana, browse key services, and contact the foundation via phone or email directly from the landing page.
